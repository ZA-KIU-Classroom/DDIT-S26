# Headline Writing Worksheet

## Purpose

Every slide in an executive presentation must have a headline that states the conclusion of that slide. Not a topic label. A conclusion.

This worksheet helps teams practice converting topic labels into conclusion headlines.

---

## Examples

| Topic label (not acceptable) | Conclusion headline (executive standard) |
|---|---|
| Industry Overview | Retail banking in Georgia faces structural disruption: three fintech entrants have grown to 2.1M users with 60% lower cost structures than incumbents |
| AI Use Cases | Two AI use cases deliver combined 22M GEL in annual cost reduction with 14-month payback in the base case |
| Strategic Options | Option 2 is the only option that addresses both the cost structure gap and the customer experience deficit within 24 months |
| Risk Assessment | Three risks are material: data governance, regulatory response, and change management; all manageable with proposed mitigations |
| ROI Model | Base case: 18-month payback, 34% IRR, 47M GEL NPV over 5 years; downside breaks even within 30 months |
