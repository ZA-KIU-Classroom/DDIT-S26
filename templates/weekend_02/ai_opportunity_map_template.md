# AI Opportunity Map Template

## Purpose

This template maps AI use cases to value chain activities and evaluates them on business value potential and implementation readiness. The output is a prioritized set of AI initiatives sequenced for your transformation roadmap.

## Instructions

Complete during the Saturday AI Opportunity Mapping workshop. Use your Weekend 1 Competitive Landscape and value chain analysis as inputs. Use the capability vocabulary from the Saturday morning teaching: prediction, generation, synthesis, agent workflow.
